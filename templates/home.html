About the Project

Our Image Processing Platform is a user-friendly web application that combines the power of advanced image processing with the simplicity of a sleek and intuitive interface. Whether you're an amateur photographer, a creative designer, or just someone who loves playing with images, this platform is designed for you.

The heart of our platform lies in its ability to enhance your images through parallel processing. This means you can apply multiple filters simultaneously, giving your images a unique and artistic touch. Additionally, our platform goes beyond basic image editing by incorporating machine learning for image classification. This allows you to gain insights into the content of your images, adding a new dimension to your exploration.

We understand the importance of simplicity, and that's why we've made the entire process from uploading to processing as easy as possible. You don't need to be a tech wizard to make the most out of your images â€“ our platform is here to make image processing accessible to everyone.

Key Features

  • Image Upload: Easily upload high-resolution images from your computer or smartphone.
  • Parallel Processing: Harness the power of parallel processing to apply multiple filters simultaneously.
  • Image Classification: Utilizing advanced machine learning models, our platform provides accurate image classification.

How It Works

Getting started is a breeze! Select the images you want to enhance, explore filters, and take advantage of our machine learning capabilities to classify the content of your images. It's that simple!

Technologies Used

Our platform is built on a robust stack of web technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and JS for a responsive and interactive user interface. HTMX for seamless server-side interactions.
  • Backend: Python and Django for a scalable and maintainable backend infrastructure. SQLite database for efficient data storage and retrieval.
